Conway's Game of Life

    Conways Game of Life Rules: Any live cell with less than 2 live neighbors dies, as if caused by underpopulation. Any live cell with more than 3 live neighbors dies, as if by overcrowding. Any live cell with 2 or 3 live neighbors lives on to the next generation. Any dead cell with exactly 3 live neighbors becomes a live cell. Click on specific cells to seed a generation, or use one of the Preset Boards.






Update Interval (ms): 100

Conway's Game of Life

    Conways Game of Life Rules: Any live cell with less than 2 live neighbours dies, as if caused by underpopulation. Any live cell with more than 3 live neighbours dies, as if by overcrowding. Any live cell with 2 or 3 live neighbours lives on to the next generation. Any dead cell with exactly 3 live neighbours becomes a live cell. Click on specific cells to seed a generation, or use one of the Preset Boards.


Update Interval (ms): 100
